Booking Attractions About Português

Explore the area!

We offer an amazing range of attractions that will connect you with nature and make you fall in love with this piece of heaven. Take a look at some of them!

trekking tours

We organise guided trekking tours around the beautiful Atlantic forest where you can find hidden waterfalls and the famously magical natural pools of Cachadaço. For more information about prices and availability please contact Reception.

boat tours

We organise guided trekking tours around the beautiful Atlantic forest where you can find hidden waterfalls and the famously magical natural pools of Cachadaço. For more information about prices and availability please contact Reception.

restaurant reservations

In the village there are many restaurants with diverse cuisine. The area's specialty is fresh seafood and Traditional Brazilian food. For more information, recommendations and reservations please contact Reception.

airport pick up/ drop off

We can organise a car / bus to pick you up and drop you off at the airport of São Paulo or Rio. The trip takes roughly 4 hours. For more information, recommendations and reservations please contact Reception.

Rental of water sport equipment

We offer our guests the possibility to rent out anything they might need for an active day at sea. From snorkeling to surfing! For more information about prices and availability please contact Reception.
