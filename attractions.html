Booking Attractions About Português

Explore the area!

We offer an amazing range of attractions that will connect you with nature and make you fall in love with this piece of heaven. Take a look at some of them!

stunning beaches

Trindade offers some of the most beautiful beaches in Brazil, and all of them at a walking distance, either by a trekking trail or a short walk. You won't believe your eyes once you see the stunning blue waters!

snorkeling

Amongst another activities, snorkeling in the crystal clear waters are most definitely on the to-do list when in Trindade. The natural pools offer a unique experience where you're going to swim with fishes and, if you're lucky, turtles might join you for a dive!

atlantic rainforest

Experience the Atlantic Rainforest in one (or many) trekking trails around Trindade. The oportunity of being so close to this tropical paradise provides an incredible conection with nature and you're going to feel relaxed and alive.

waterfalls

There's many waterfalls on a trail distance from the village, which gives our guests the opportunity of finding adventures that suit the most diverse needs. Special mention to the Pedra Que Engole, a river rock where adventurous bathers slide through a hole and into a natural pool on the other side!
