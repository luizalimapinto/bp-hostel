Welcome to the Jungle!

TThe BP Hostel is the younger sister of the Bicho-Preguiça BnB, a well known and established hosting facility in Trindade, Brazil. We believe that our love for our guests and our little village provides an incredible atmosphere and an experience you’ll never forget. Focusing on preserving the beautiful Atlantic rainforest we’re based on, we reinforce environmentally friendly practices and intend to keep the hostel every day more sustainable and green.

Rooms Facilities

Hostel Bicho Preguiça Hostel offers beautiful rooms with the access to kitchen, inside porch and outdoor area. The property is well furnished and decorated with rugs, paintings Brazilian culture.
