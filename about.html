This is BP hostel

The BP Hostel is the younger sister of the Bicho-Preguiça BnB, a well known and established hosting facility in Trindade, Brazil. We believe that our love for our guests and our little village provides an incredible atmosphere and an experience you’ll never forget. Focusing on preserving the beautiful Atlantic rainforest we’re based on, we reinforce environmentally friendly practices and intend to keep the hostel every day more sustainable and green.

The owner, Priscila, is a civil engineer that decided to leave behind the construction life and dedicate herself to build her dream, the BP Hostel. With a great love for MPB (brazilian popular music), Priscila decided to decorate the place with influences from the Tropicália, the musical and political movement that arose in the late 60s in Brazil due to the military coup the country suffered.

Now, also considering the political tension Brazil suffers at the moment, the Hostel intends to be a place where diversity, freedom and happiness are not only accepted but encourage.
